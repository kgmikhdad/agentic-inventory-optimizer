# Supplier Policy

Supplier lead time must be considered before approving reorder quantity. Longer lead times require higher safety stock because replenishment takes more time.

Minimum order quantity must be respected when placing orders. If a supplier requires a minimum order quantity, the purchase order should either meet the minimum or not be placed.

Pack-size constraints must be respected. For example, if a product is supplied in packs of 20, the reorder quantity should be a multiple of 20.

When warehouse capacity is limited, products with the highest expected stockout penalty per storage unit should be prioritized.
